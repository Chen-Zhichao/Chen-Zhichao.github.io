Hello, I am Zhichao Chen (陈治潮), a third-year Ph.D. student at [NanoCAD Lab](https://nanocad.ee.ucla.edu/), [UCLA ECE](https://www.ee.ucla.edu/), advised by Prof. [Puneet Gupta](https://samueli.ucla.edu/people/puneet-gupta/). 
Prior to that, I received my B.E. degree from E I was working at [ISCL](https://iscl.nju.edu.cn/), [Nanjing](https://www.nju.edu.cn/en/).
My research focuses on system technology co-optimization (STCO) and advanced packaging. Currently I am working on developing a yield modeling framework for hybrid bonding technology.
